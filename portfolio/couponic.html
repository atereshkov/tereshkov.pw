Couponic

Couponic is an application that allows users to see discount coupons from various establishments (restaurants, shops, cinemas, airports) on the move, collect and use them. The app helps to reach the desired place by using mobile application navigator and also can simultaneously support discount and advertising campaigns in different cities/regions.
